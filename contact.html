Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit my own recipe to PlateOnRepeat?

We love featuring recipes from our community! You can submit your recipe by clicking on "Submit Recipe" in your account dashboard. Our editorial team reviews all submissions for quality and adherence to our guidelines before publishing. Please include clear instructions, ingredient measurements, and at least one high-quality photo.

How can I report an error in a recipe?

If you've found an error in one of our recipes, please email us at corrections@plateonrepeat.com with the recipe title and details about the error. We appreciate your help in keeping our recipes accurate and will review your report as soon as possible.

Do you offer nutrition information for your recipes?

We're working on adding nutrition information to all recipes. Currently, some recipes include approximate nutritional values calculated by our system. These should be used as estimates only. For specific dietary needs, we recommend consulting with a nutritionist or using a specialized nutrition calculator with your exact ingredients.

How do I cancel my premium subscription?

You can cancel your premium subscription at any time by going to your Account Settings and selecting "Subscription." Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing period. You'll continue to have access to premium features until that date. For any issues with cancellation, please contact our support team.

Can I use your recipes in my cookbook/blog/restaurant menu?

Our recipes are protected by copyright, but we're often happy to grant permission for non-commercial use with proper attribution. For commercial uses (including blogs with advertising), please contact us at licensing@plateonrepeat.com with details about your project. Many of our recipes are adaptations of classic dishes that you're welcome to reinterpret with your own creative twist!
